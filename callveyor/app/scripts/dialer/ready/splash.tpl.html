Pre-flight checks

  1. Computer has a built-in microphone or a headset is plugged in
  2. Internet speed received a 'B' or better from pingtest.net
  3. Firewall(s) allow Voice over IP (VoIP) connections. Some firewalls call this 'Skype'. Read more...

Poor voice quality, dropped calls or connecting/disconnecting calls rapidly are symptoms of a mis-configured firewall or poor network conditions. Try the Other Phone path.
